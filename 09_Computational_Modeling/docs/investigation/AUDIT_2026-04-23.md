The 95% CI is percentiles over 50 draws of arbitrary 30% jitter, not true uncertainty (which would include the 30× scaling choice, post-extraction kinetics uncertainty, correlation structure). Abstract's "95% CI 4.0–6.0" implies statistical rigor the method doesn't provide.
