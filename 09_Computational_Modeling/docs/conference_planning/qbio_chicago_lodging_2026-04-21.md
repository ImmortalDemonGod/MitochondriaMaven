## Priority Order

### 1. UChicago Summer Conference Housing — CONTACT FIRST
- **Cost:** ~$40-80/night (estimated — includes dining)
- **Distance:** On campus. Walk to BSLC.
- **Contact:** summerconferences@uchicago.edu
- **URL:** housing.uchicago.edu/summer-conferences/
- **Notes:** Available mid-June through late August. Single/double occupancy, shared bathrooms, A/C, 24/7 front desk, laundry. Dining (breakfast/lunch/dinner) at Woodlawn Dining Commons. Email soon — fills up. Not guaranteed for non-UChicago attendees but worth asking.

### 2. La Quinta Inn & Suites Hyde Park
- **Cost:** $67-98/night
- **Distance:** 5.3 miles, 15-20 min by CTA
- **Book via:** Expedia or direct
- **Notes:** Budget-friendly, Hyde Park area, under $100 target.

### 3. HI Chicago Hostel (private room)
- **Cost:** $76-111/night (private room with ensuite)
- **Distance:** South Loop — CTA to campus
- **URL:** hostelworld.com or booking.com (search "HI Chicago J. Ira & Nicki Harris")
- **Notes:** South Loop location. Private rooms available. Good value, legitimate hostel.

### 4. Airbnb Hyde Park
- **Cost:** $80-150/night (July peak season)
- **Distance:** Walking distance to campus
- **URL:** airbnb.com — search Hyde Park, Chicago, Jul 27-30
- **Notes:** Studios and private rooms available near campus. Check early — July fills fast.

### 5. Best Western Plus Hyde Park
- **Cost:** $129-166/night
- **Distance:** 5.3 miles, 15-20 min
- **URL:** universityofchicagohotel.com
- **Notes:** Free parking, free breakfast, Lake Michigan views. Slightly over $100 target but includes breakfast.

### 6. Motel 6 South Side (budget fallback)
- **Cost:** $43-56/night
- **Distance:** South side — longer CTA commute
- **URL:** motel6.com
- **Notes:** Cheapest hotel option. Longer commute. Fine if cost is the only constraint.

### Lodging Strategy
Sleep in Jackson Park — wooded areas near the lagoon, 8 min walk to BSLC. Low foot traffic at night.

**Fallback shelters (call ahead):**
- Door of Hope Rescue Mission — 5313 S Indiana Ave, 1.5 mi from BSLC. Men 25+, free, meals + showers included. **(773) 624-0586**
- SPARC — 2241 S Halsted. 24/7, call 311.

### Morning Routine
1. Wake up, pack everything
2. Shower at Anytime Fitness or South Side YMCA (6330 S Stony Island Ave)
3. Change into conference clothes (kept sealed in dry bag)
4. Walk 8 min to BSLC
5. Charge all devices at conference venue during sessions

### Gear List
- Lightweight tarp or mylar emergency blanket
- Dry bag — keep conference clothes clean and sealed
- Rain poncho — July storms roll in fast off the lake
- Wet wipes (large pack) — field hygiene
- Power bank — charge at BSLC during sessions
- Money belt — ID, cash, phone on body at all times
- Download Google Maps offline before leaving OKC

### Valuables During the Day
Laptop and gear stay with you at the conference. Verify whether BSLC or HI Chicago hostel has day-use lockers if needed.

### Food Resources (all walking distance from BSLC)
- **Love Fridge** — 5200 S Hyde Park Blvd, 24/7, free
- **Kenwood UCC Soup Kitchen** — Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri 9am-1pm, free hot meals
- **First Presbyterian Food Market** — 6400 S Kimbark, Thu 10am-12pm
- **Aldi** — cheapest grocery backup
